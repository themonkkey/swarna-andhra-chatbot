What is GVA — and Why Does Your District Own It?

GVA = Output − Intermediate Consumption

GDDP = GVA + Taxes − Subsidies

NATIONAL

MoSPI compiles GVA from all sectors using production data, NSS surveys, ASI (Annual Survey of Industries), and corporate filings (MCA21).

▶

STATE (AP)

APDES apportions national GVA to Andhra Pradesh using state-level activity indicators — production volumes, employment, expenditure.

▶

DISTRICT

State GVA is split to 28 districts using district-level PROXY indicators — factory electricity, vehicle registrations, GST collections, Udyam units, tourist arrivals.

KEY INSIGHT FOR DISTRICT OFFICERS

District GVA is NOT directly measured — it is ESTIMATED from proxy indicators that YOU control.

More Udyam registrations → higher MSME GVA. More industrial electricity connections → higher Manufacturing GVA. More tourist arrivals → higher Trade GVA.

This means: every real economic action in your district directly improves your DDP number.
