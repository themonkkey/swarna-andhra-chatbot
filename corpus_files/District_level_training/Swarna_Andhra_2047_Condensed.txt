IMPLEMENTATION GOVERNANCE

How the Vision Will Be Governed

Four-tier structure. District officials operate the bottom two tiers — where strategy becomes service.

Leadership Council

Sets the vision; approves high-level plans; ensures alignment with broader state goals.

Steering Committee

Chaired by Chief Secretary; coordinates theme task forces; translates strategy into action.

State VMU + Theme Task Forces

Within Planning Dept; coordinates districts, monitors progress, assesses needs. Focus areas: P4, Zero Poverty, Blue Economy, Knowledge & Innovation.

District VMUs + Line Departments ◀ YOU ARE HERE

The execution engine: outcome-based management, KPIs, SOPs, nodal officers owning each action item.

Information flows UP: Family → Village → Mandal → District → State, with AI and defined KPIs at each level

Slide 12: The four-tier governance structure. Emphasise that district officers are at tier 4 — the execution engine. The information flows upward, but resources and guidance flow downward. The district is the hinge that makes this loop function.

THE METHOD

A Five-Step District Playbook

STEP

1

Diagnose

Baseline your district against 2047 KPIs. Identify the biggest gaps in income, jobs, health, education.

STEP

2

Prioritise

Pick sectors where your district has the strongest endowment or widest gap. Focus beats spread.

STEP

3

Plan

Build a District Development Plan with ≥10 bankable projects, each mapped to a Padi Sutralu and a KPI.

STEP

4

Deliver

Assign nodal officers, define SOPs, sequence works, unblock land & clearances, convene line departments.

STEP

5

Monitor

Track KPIs on a live dashboard; review monthly; course-correct with data and citizen feedback.

Photograph this slide. This is the operating method.

Slide 13: The five-step method is the practical takeaway of the entire training. Walk through each step. Note that Step 2 (Prioritise) is the hardest — districts with many endowments try to do everything and execute nothing. Step 5 (Monitor) is where most districts fail — the dashboard must be live, not a periodic report.
