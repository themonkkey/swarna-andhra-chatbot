THE IMPLEMENTATION SPINE

How the Vision Will Be Governed

The state vision is delivered through a four-tier structure. District officials operate the bottom two tiers — where strategy becomes service.

Leadership Council

Sets the vision; approves high-level plans; ensures alignment with broader state goals.

Steering Committee

Chaired by the Chief Secretary; coordinates theme task forces; translates strategy into action.

State VMU + Theme Task Forces

Within Planning Dept; coordinates districts, monitors progress, assesses needs. P4, Zero Poverty, Blue Economy, Knowledge & Innovation.

District VMUs + Line Departments

The execution engine: outcome-based management, KPIs, SOPs, nodal officers owning each action item.

Information flows up: Family → Village → Mandal → District → State, with AI and defined KPIs at each level.
